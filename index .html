About

I am a postdoctoral researcher in experimental quantum materials science. My research connects materials growth, structural characterization, electronic structure measurements, magnetic imaging, and scientific data analysis.

My background includes molecular beam epitaxy, laser-assisted synthesis of 2D materials, Raman spectroscopy, XPS, momentum microscopy, ARPES-style band mapping, nanoscale imaging, and AI-assisted analysis workflows.

Research Focus

Quantum Materials Growth

Epitaxial growth of thin films and heterostructures using MBE and related synthesis methods.

Advanced Characterization

AI-Guided Discovery

Scientific software and data workflows for reproducible, metadata-rich, and AI-assisted experiments.
